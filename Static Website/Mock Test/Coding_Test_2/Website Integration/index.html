Animals

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles are reptiles of the order Testudines.

Raccoon

Raccoons are medium-sized mammals native to North America.

Kangaroo

Kangaroos are large marsupials found only in Australia.

Lion

Lions are large cats native to Africa and India.

Panda

Pandas are bear-like animals native to China, known for their distinctive black-and-white fur.
